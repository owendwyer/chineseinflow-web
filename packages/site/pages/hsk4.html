HSK 4

HSK 4 introduces 600 new characters, bringing the cumulative total to 1,200 simplified Chinese characters. Learners at this level can discuss a range of topics and explain viewpoints in Chinese. HSK 4 is roughly equivalent to CEFR B1. Audio is not included for HSK 4 and 5 in the game.

Practice HSK 4 vocabulary in the Chinese In Flow game. Make sure you are comfortable with HSK 3 and lower levels before tackling this set.
