Hanping Chinese Dictionary

Hanping Chinese Dictionary is a long-running, offline-first Chinese dictionary app developed by an independent developer and used by over one million learners worldwide. It is particularly well known for treating Cantonese as a first-class language, rather than as an add-on to Mandarin.

Official site: hanpingchinese.com

Platforms: Android · iOS

Two separate apps: Mandarin and Cantonese

Hanping Chinese Dictionary (Mandarin)

  • Modern Mandarin (Putonghua)
  • Pinyin with tone marks or numbers
  • English ↔ Chinese lookup
  • Offline dictionary data

Hanping Cantonese Dictionary

  • Native Cantonese focus (not Mandarin-with-Cantonese-pronunciation)
  • Jyutping romanisation
  • Cantonese-specific vocabulary and readings
  • Offline dictionary data
Important: Many “Cantonese” dictionary apps are actually Mandarin dictionaries with Cantonese readings added on top. Hanping Cantonese is a dedicated Cantonese dictionary with its own data, search logic, and phonology.

Key features

  • Fast offline search (characters, pronunciation, or English)
  • Configurable Simplified / Traditional character display modes
  • Star words and build custom word lists
  • Radical lookup and character structure information
  • Uses Unihan data for detailed character metadata
  • No account required for core dictionary use

Pricing

Hanping is free to download and use. Optional in-app purchases are available for advanced features such as backup and restore.

Who is Hanping for?

  • Serious learners of Mandarin or Cantonese
  • Cantonese learners who want a dictionary built specifically for Cantonese
  • Users who want fast, offline access without subscriptions
  • Advanced learners interested in character structure and phonology
