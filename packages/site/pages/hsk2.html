HSK 2

HSK 2 builds on HSK 1 with another 150 characters, for a total of 300 simplified Chinese characters. Learners at this level can handle simple, direct communication about daily life. HSK 1 and 2 together are roughly equivalent to CEFR A1.

Practice the full HSK 2 set in the Chinese In Flow game. If you have not covered the basics yet, start with HSK 1 characters first.
