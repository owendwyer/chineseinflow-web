HSK 2

This application includes content for HSK 2

HSK two is the second level for the HSK test and is again pretty easy. To be at HSK level two you need to be able to recognize and use the first 300 characters.

This level is defined as being for people starting out who can use Chinese in a simple and direct manner and using vocab for daily life. Both HSK One and Two are roughly equivalent to level A1 in the CEFR.
