HSK 5

HSK 5 is an advanced level with 1,300 new characters, for 2,500 characters in total across all HSK levels. At HSK 5 you can read Chinese newspapers, websites, and give speeches or hold in-depth discussions. This level is roughly equivalent to CEFR B2.

Chinese In Flow includes HSK 5 character sets in the free matching game (without audio). If you are working up to this level, practice HSK 4 and the lower levels first.
