HSK 3

HSK 3 adds 300 new characters on top of HSK 1 and 2, for 600 characters in total. At this level you can use Chinese in everyday personal and work situations. HSK 3 is roughly equivalent to CEFR A2.

All HSK 3 vocabulary is available in the Chinese In Flow matching game. Review HSK 1 and HSK 2 first if you are still building your foundation.
